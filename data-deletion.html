3. How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Automatic Deletion (Recommended)

Simply uninstall Profitly from your Shopify store. Our system automatically detects the uninstall and initiates data deletion within 48 hours.

Steps to uninstall:
  1. Log in to your Shopify Admin
  2. Go to Settings → Apps and sales channels
  3. Find Profitly in your installed apps
  4. Click Uninstall
  5. Confirm the uninstallation
What happens automatically:
  • All order data, product data, and analytics are queued for deletion
  • Connected advertising integrations (Meta, Google, TikTok) are disconnected
  • Your account and settings are permanently removed
  • You receive a confirmation email within 48 hours

Method 2: Manual Request

If you need immediate deletion or want to verify your data has been removed, contact us directly:

Email us at: hello@bishoplabs.com

Include in your email:
  • Your Shopify store domain (e.g., yourstore.myshopify.com)
  • The email address associated with your Shopify account
  • Confirmation that you want all data deleted

We will process your request within 24 hours and send you a confirmation email once complete.

4. What Data Is Deleted

When you request deletion, we permanently remove:

All Stored Data:

  • Order Data: All order information, revenue data, and transaction history
  • Product Data: COGS data, product costs, and product catalog information
  • Store Information: Your store details, plan selection, and settings
  • Analytics: All calculated metrics, reports, and insights
  • Connected Accounts: Meta, Google, and TikTok advertising connections
  • Alert Settings: Threshold configurations and notification preferences
  • User Account: Your account record and authentication data

What We Retain (Briefly):

For legal and security purposes only:
  • Billing Records: Invoice and payment data for 7 years (tax/legal requirement)
  • Deletion Request Log: Record that deletion was requested (email and date only, for 90 days)
  • Security Logs: IP addresses and access logs for 30 days (security compliance)

These minimal records contain no business data, customer data, or analytics.

5. Data Deletion Confirmation

After deletion is complete, you will receive an email confirmation containing:

  • Confirmation that your data has been permanently deleted
  • The date and time of deletion
  • A reference ID for your records
  • Contact information if you have questions

6. Facebook/Meta Data Deletion

As a Facebook/Meta Business Partner, we comply with Meta's Data Deletion Requirements:

Delete Meta/Facebook Data

To delete data associated with your Meta advertising accounts:

  1. Go to Profitly → Settings → Integrations
  2. Click Disconnect next to Meta/Facebook
  3. This immediately removes your Meta access token and stops data synchronization
  4. All cached Meta ad data is deleted within 24 hours

Alternatively: Uninstalling the App automatically disconnects all advertising integrations and deletes all associated data within 48 hours.

7. GDPR "Right to be Forgotten"

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to be forgotten under GDPR. We honor this request:

  • No questions asked
  • Deletion within 30 days maximum (typically within 48 hours)
  • Complete removal of all personal and business data
  • Written confirmation provided

8. Data Backup and Recovery

Important: Once deletion is complete, data cannot be recovered. We do not keep backups of deleted user data. If you think you might need your analytics in the future, we recommend:
  • Exporting your reports before uninstalling
  • Saving key metrics to your own systems

9. What If I Reinstall Profitly?

If you reinstall Profitly after deletion:

  • All previous data is gone - we start fresh
  • Your store data will resync from Shopify (orders, products)
  • You will need to reconfigure COGS and settings
  • Connected advertising accounts must be reauthorized
  • Trial eligibility resets (if applicable)

10. Questions About Data Deletion

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I delete only my advertising data but keep the app?
A: Yes. Go to Settings → Integrations and disconnect any advertising platforms. This removes the connection and deletes cached ad data.

Q: How do I know my data is really deleted?
A: You receive a confirmation email. If you have concerns, email us and we can provide a deletion certificate.

Q: Does deleting Profitly delete my Shopify data?
A: No. Your Shopify store data (orders, products, customers) remains in Shopify. We only delete the data we stored for analytics.

Q: Can I request partial deletion?
A: Currently, we only support complete account deletion. You cannot selectively delete certain data types.
