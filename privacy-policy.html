Privacy Policy

Profitly - Shopify Profit Analytics

Effective Date: April 2, 2026
Last Updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Profitly ("we," "our," or "us"). Profitly is a Shopify application that provides profit analytics and business intelligence for e-commerce merchants. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Shopify app and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Shopify Store Data

When you install Profitly, we collect the following information through Shopify's API:

  • Store Information: Store name, domain, Shopify plan, and store owner contact information
  • Order Data: Order details, revenue, refunds, shipping costs, taxes, and payment fees
  • Product Data: Product titles, variant IDs, prices, and cost of goods sold (COGS) that you input
  • Customer Data: Limited customer information necessary for cohort analysis (order counts, not personal details)

2.2 Advertising Platform Data (With Your Consent)

When you choose to connect advertising accounts, we collect:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads: Ad spend, campaign performance, and account IDs
  • Google Ads: Ad spend, campaign metrics, and account information
  • TikTok Ads: Ad spend and campaign performance data

2.3 Usage Data

  • App usage statistics and feature interactions
  • Error logs and performance data (for troubleshooting)
  • IP address and browser information (for security)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Calculate profit margins and provide analytics dashboards
  • Generate business insights and reports
  • Send you profit alerts and notifications (only when configured)
  • Improve our app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage

  • All data is stored on secure cloud servers (AWS/DigitalOcean) with encryption at rest
  • Database connections use SSL/TLS encryption
  • We retain data only as long as necessary for service provision

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Automated backups with encrypted storage

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We only share data with:

  • Shopify: For app functionality and compliance (required by Shopify platform)
  • Cloud Hosting Providers: For infrastructure (AWS/DigitalOcean) under strict data processing agreements
  • Analytics Tools: Anonymous usage statistics only (no personal or business data)

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • You can access all your data through the Profitly dashboard
  • You can modify or delete your COGS data at any time
  • You can disconnect advertising integrations anytime

6.2 Data Deletion

You can request complete data deletion by:

  • Uninstalling the app from your Shopify store (automatic data deletion within 48 hours)
  • Contacting us directly for immediate deletion

See our Data Deletion Policy for detailed instructions.

6.3 GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the EU, you have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Data portability
  • Object to data processing

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: For session management and security
  • Preference cookies: To remember your dashboard settings
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage tracking (optional, can be disabled)

8. Children's Privacy

Profitly is not intended for use by children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of Profitly after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Compliance

Profitly complies with:

  • Shopify App Store requirements
  • Facebook/Meta Platform Policies
  • Google API Services User Data Policy
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
